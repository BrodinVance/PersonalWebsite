## Story
- A god has died, causing its essence to spread over a large area
- In search of this valuable essence and the effects it has on the world, towns have been built in safe areas.
- Many areas, where the essence was initially more concentrated, are more dangerous
- Over time, this essence has faded, but some remains
- A singularity/point where the heart of the god lays
- Brine is the essence that leaks from the god
- World will be called Pangea



## Protagonist
- Little Guy
- Not Human - `Original in-game` species
- Born in the area near where the god died - it's why they are the only one able to go near the heart
